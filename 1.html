In the year 2092. Earth has become nearly uninhabitable due to tree loss and increased desertification. Fleeing the sick earth, the UTS corporation builds a new orbiting home for humanity that mimics the natural processes on Earth. However, only a chosen few are permitted to ascend and become UTS citizens, while those remaining on Earth breathe in polluted air and fight to feed their families while struggling through frequent natural and man-made calamities. Under guidance from the UTS founder and CEO, James Sullivan, the company is working towards making Mars a new Earth, growing genetically modified plants on the planet and turning it into a hospitable environment for humans.
